About Jean Grey:

Jean Grey is a powerful mutant gifted with telepathy and telekinesis. She is one of the original members of the X-Men and is known for her strong heart and compassion.

But beneath her calm and gentle personality lies an immense cosmic force — The Phoenix — that grants her power beyond imagination.

Her story is more than just strength; it’s about control, sacrifice, and the battle between light and darkness within herself. Jean Grey reminds us that even the strongest hearts can burn, but they can also rise again from the ashes.

Jean: “I am fire and life incarnate. Now and forever I am Phoenix.”
