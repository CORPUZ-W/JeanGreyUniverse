Movie Reviews
Over the years, Jean Grey has appeared in several X-Men movies — each revealing a different side of her journey, from her early days as a young mutant to her dramatic transformation into the Dark Phoenix.

Here are some of the most iconic films where Jean’s power and humanity shine.

Title: X-Men: The Last Stand (2006)

Portrayed by: Famke Janssen

Review:

› In this film, Jean Grey’s struggle with her inner Phoenix power takes center stage. It shows how dangerous uncontrollable power can be — but also how deeply human Jean remains.

🌟 Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5) — Emotional and tragic, with a strong performance.

Title: X-Men: Apocalypse (2016)

Portrayed by: Sophie Turner

Review:

› We see a younger Jean discovering her abilities and finding her place in the X-Men. The movie hints at her connection to the Phoenix Force.

🌟 Rating: ★★★☆☆ (3/5) — Exciting introduction to her power, though the story feels rushed.

Title: Dark Phoenix (2019)

Portrayed by: Sophie Turner

Review:

› This movie dives into Jean’s emotional conflict and the danger of her cosmic power. It shows both her strength and vulnerability as she tries to control the Phoenix within.

🌟 Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5) — Visually powerful and emotional, focusing on Jean’s inner battle.
