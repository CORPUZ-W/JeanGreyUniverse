Jean Grey Phoenix Force Profile
Jean Grey's Information

Full Name: Jean Elaine Grey-Summers

Alias: Marvel Girl, Phoenix, Dark Phoenix

Affiliation: X-Men

Species: Mutant

First Appearance: X-Men #1 (1963)

Created By: Stan Lee and Jack Kirby

Occupation: Superhero, Teacher, Mutant Leader

Place of Origin: Annandale-on-Hudson, New York, USA

Host Of: The Phoenix Force

Personality

Jean Grey is known for her kind, selfless, and brave personality. She deeply cares for her friends and always stands up for what she believes in. As one of the most trusted members of the X-Men, Jean serves as both a guide and an inspiration to others. Even when facing overwhelming power or conflict, she stays true to her heart and her values.

Jean Grey Powers and Abilities
  • Telepathy – She can read minds, communicate mentally, and sense emotions.
  • Telekinesis – Jean can move and control objects with her mind, create force fields, and even fly.
  • Astral Projection – She can separate her spirit from her body to travel through the astral plane.
  • Psychic Shield – Protecting herself and allies from mental attacks.
  • Phoenix Force (when bonded) – Her powers expand to a cosmic level. She can control energy, manipulate matter, heal instantly, and rise again after death.
  • Empathy – She can feel and calm the emotions of others.
